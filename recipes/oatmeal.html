Oatmeal

Description

Start your day with a warm bowl of a breakfast classic. This simple oatmeal recipe is the perfect option for those wanting a quick and satisfying breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of rolled oats
  • 3/4 cup of water
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • Salt
  • Cinnamon
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Add 1/2 cup of rolled oats into a microwave-safe bowl
  2. Add 3/4 cup of water and 1/4 cup of milk into bowl
  3. Add a pinch of salt
  4. Microwave on high for 1 minute and 45 seconds
  5. Add cinnamon and honey to taste
  6. Enjoy!
